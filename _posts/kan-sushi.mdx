The strength in this omakase meal were the fishes. Not only was it exciting to try so many different varieties of fishes, the sushi themselves allowed the natural flavours of the fishes to shine. Coupled with the great value for money of Kan Sushi's Lunch omakase options , this is definitely an omakase spot I will be returning to!
